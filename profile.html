Red Velvet

Debut on August 1, 2014

Red Velvet is a popular South Korean girl group in the K-pop (Korean pop) music industry. The group is known for its unique and versatile music style, which combines various genres such as pop, R&B, electronic, and hip-hop. Red Velvet has gained widespread recognition for their catchy songs, striking visuals, and captivating performances
>




MEMBERS



Irene

Birthname: Bae Joo-hyun

She was born on March 29, 1991, in Daegu, South Korea.

Leader of Red Velvet

Primarily serves as the group's rapper, contributing to their songs with her rap verses. She is also known for her sweet and soothing vocal tone, which she occasionally showcases in certain songs and performances.

Irene is often recognized as one of the most visually striking and iconic idols in K-pop. Her beauty, style, and fashion sense have earned her a large fan following, and she is regarded as a fashion icon in the industry.



Wendy

Birthname: Son Seung-wan

She was born on February 21, 1994, in Seoul, South Korea, but she grew up in Canada

Main Vocalist of Red Velvet

Her impressive vocal range and ability to convey emotion through her singing make her a standout member of the group. She is often responsible for the group's high notes and vocal ad-libs in their songs

In addition to her work with Red Velvet, Wendy has pursued solo projects. She released her debut solo mini-album titled "Like Water" in April 2021. The album showcased her vocal prowess and was well-received by fans and critics



Seulgi

Birthname: Kang Seulgi

She was born on February 10, 1994, in Ansan, South Korea.

Main dance of Red Velvet

While primarily recognized for her dancing, Seulgi is also a talented vocalist and contributes to Red Velvet's harmonies and vocal performances.

Seulgi is known for her versatility and adaptability, which allows her to excel in various concepts and styles that Red Velvet explores in their music and performances.



Joy

Birthname: Park Soo-young

She was born on September 3, 1996, in Jeju, South Korea

Sub-Vocalist of Red Velvet

She contributes to the group's vocal performances and harmonies.

In addition to her music career, Joy has pursued acting and has appeared in various South Korean dramas, including "The Liar and His Lover," "Tempted," and "The Great Seducer." Her acting talents have been recognized and praised by viewers and critics.



Yeri

Birthname: Kim Yeri.

She was born on March 5, 1999, in Seoul, South Korea.

Yeri is a versatile performer, contributing to Red Velvet as both a vocalist and rapper. Her role within the group includes providing harmonies and rapping in certain songs.

She has pursued individual projects, including collaborating with other artists and participating in OST (Original Soundtrack) projects for South Korean dramas.
